# **App Name**: Hackathon Horizon

## Core Features:

- Secure Team Leader Authentication: Allows hackathon participants to securely sign up and log in as team leaders using their email and password.
- Leader & Team Member Profile Management: A comprehensive form for leaders to input their details (name, email, phone, college, degree, branch, LinkedIn, GitHub) and optionally add up to three team members, pre-filling college/degree/branch based on the leader's details.
- AI-Powered Welcome Email Tool: Upon successful registration, an AI tool automatically drafts and sends a personalized welcome email to the team leader's registered email address.
- Project Submission Interface: Provides a dedicated area for team leaders to upload their PPT in PDF format, enter a problem statement, and write an abstract text, with a clear deadline display for April 6th.
- Database Persistence for Registrations & Submissions: Stores all participant data (leader, team members) and project submission details (PPT PDF, problem statement, abstract) securely in a database.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary action color: A vibrant electric blue (#1C66E6) to represent innovation and clarity, offering a striking contrast against a dark background.
- Background color: A very dark, subtle bluish-gray (#191E25), creating a modern, focused dark theme suitable for technology events.
- Accent color: A light, energetic aqua (#AEF0FA), providing strong contrast for highlights, alerts, and secondary interactive elements.
- Headline and body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its clean, modern, and highly legible appearance, which supports a wide range of content in a technological context.
- Utilize sleek, minimalist line icons to maintain a clean and professional aesthetic that complements the technological theme.
- Employ a responsive, form-centric layout with ample negative space to reduce visual clutter and enhance user focus on input fields and submission steps.
- Implement subtle, fluid animations for form transitions and button feedback, enhancing user experience without distracting from the primary task of registration and submission.
